About our Cafe

Our Cafe is an Italian style cafe that serves simple, organic food – including Vegan and Gluten Free options – accompanied by aromatic coffee and delicious tea. We do things differently here and we pay the utmost attention to the quality of our products. Our Cafe is a hybrid combining a strong coffee culture and a robust food culture under one roof in a welcoming, cozy and comfortable neighborhood cafe.

Our Coffee

We serve a wild range of coffee drinks made from the finest coffee beans, roasted in Italy, and our baristas really do know their beans. With their superior techniques and passion for brewing amazing coffee, they will make you sure you get the perfect cuppa right in your hands.

Our Desserts

Our desserts are organic, made with local ingredients whenever possible to provide you with fresh, healthy and delicious meals. We have a well stocked pastry cabinet, our food has an international flavor and we offer gluten free, vegetarian and vegan options.
